 Objectives
Apply the week’s concepts through practical, hands-on tasks.

1. Use Spark or Dask to load a large dataset and inspect its schema.
2. Implement a transformation pipeline that cleans and enriches the data.
3. Compare execution performance with different partitioning strategies.
4. Build a simple API or batch job that makes use of the processed data.
5. Summarize the design choices and scalability considerations.
