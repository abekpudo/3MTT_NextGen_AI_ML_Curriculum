 Week 5 Exercises: Capstone Finalization & Deployment

 Objectives
Take your capstone from a raw dataset to a finished, deployable project with professional documentation.

 1. Final Dataset Audit
- Create a "Data Quality Report" for your chosen capstone dataset.
- List all cleaning steps applied and any remaining limitations.
- Generate a final correlation matrix to verify feature selection.

 2. The Deployment Sprint
- Use Streamlit to build a basic UI for your model.
- Include:
    - Input fields for the top 3-5 features.
    - A "Predict" button.
    - A clear display of the result (e.g., "High Risk of Churn" in red).
- Challenge: Add a chart to your Streamlit app that shows feature importance for your model.

 3. Reporting and Visualization
- Create one "Signature Chart" that summarizes your project's most important finding.
- Write an Executive Summary (max 200 words) using the "Problem-Solution-Impact" framework.

 4. Pitch Practice
- Record a 2-minute "Elevator Pitch" of your project.
- Explain:
    1. The business problem.
    2. Your technical approach.
    3. The final performance.
    4. One key recommendation for the stakeholder.

 5. Repository Cleanup
- Ensure your `README.md` is complete.
- Organize your files into `data/`, `notebooks/`, and `src/`.
- Verify that your `requirements.txt` includes all necessary libraries.
