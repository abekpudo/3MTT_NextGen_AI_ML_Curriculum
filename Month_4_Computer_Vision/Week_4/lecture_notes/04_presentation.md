Presentation

Overview
Cover best practices for communicating technical results clearly through narrative, visuals, dashboards, and executive summaries.

Why This Topic Matters
Strong reporting ensures that model results are understood and adopted by stakeholders, bridging the gap between technical findings and business decisions.

Lecture Goals
- Summarize the problem, approach, and results
- Build charts that highlight key model insights
- Explain limitations and next steps
- Prepare a crisp slide deck and demo script
- Tailor the message to technical and non-technical audiences

Key Concepts
- storytelling
- visualization
- executive summary
- insights
- communication

Classroom Example / Case Study
- Create a narrative that follows the problem-solution-impact structure
- Use tables and visualizations to compare baseline and final model performance
- Document assumptions, risks, and recommended actions

Practical Implementation Notes
- Avoid overwhelming audiences with too many technical details.
- Focus on outcomes and business value first.
- Use consistent terminology across slides and reports.

Tools & Libraries
- PowerPoint / Google Slides
- Markdown reports
- Jupyter Notebook
- Matplotlib / Seaborn
- Tableau / Power BI (optional)
