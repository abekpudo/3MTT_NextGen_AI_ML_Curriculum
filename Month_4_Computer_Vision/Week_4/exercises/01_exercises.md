 Objectives
Apply the week’s concepts through practical, hands-on tasks.

1. Load an image dataset and inspect a batch of samples.
2. Apply preprocessing and augmentation to improve robustness.
3. Train a CNN or transfer learning model for a classification task.
4. Evaluate the model and visualize prediction errors.
5. Document the dataset, model, and deployment considerations.
